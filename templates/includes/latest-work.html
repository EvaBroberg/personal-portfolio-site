Flora and Fauna

Flora and Fauna is a memory game with a goal to entertain and visually appeal to the player. Game was created for people of any age that are looking for a fun way to spend a bit of free time and are looking for a game that would not only be entertaining but would also stimulate the brain. While game is kid-friendly and easy to understand and navigate, it's primary purpose was to appeal for a grown-up who needs to add some fun into their busy schedule.
